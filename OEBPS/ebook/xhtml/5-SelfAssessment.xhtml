Self-Assessment Worksheet

Creative Identity Audit

Refining Your Toolkit & Artistic Voice

This self-assessment is designed to help you reflect on your current journey as a freelance hairstylist. Take your time to answer each question thoughtfully.

1
Current Skill Inventory: List your top 3 technical strengths (e.g., dry cutting, balayage) and 3 areas where you feel a "skill gap."
2
Burnout Check-In: On a scale of 1-10, how ignited is your creative fire right now? Describe what is currently fueling you or draining you.
3
The "Why" Rediscovery: Recall a specific moment in your career where you felt completely in your element. What were you doing? Who were you with?
4
Creative Aspirations: What sparked your passion for hairstyling? What are your biggest hopes, dreams, and aspirations for your craft?
5
Conscious Hairstyling: What does being a "conscious hairstylist" mean to you? What values do you want to bring more fully into your work?
6
Looking Ahead: What are you most excited to learn, experience, and create through the sacred art of hairstyling? What's calling to your heart?
