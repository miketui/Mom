SELF-CARE JOURNAL

Nurturing Your Creative Spirit

As hairstylists, we dedicate ourselves to the well-being and transformation of others, yet often neglect our own needs. This journal is your sacred space to prioritize self-care, recognize burnout signals, and cultivate practices that sustain your passion and energy for the demanding yet rewarding work you do.

Physical Well-Being Check-In

How is your body feeling today? Are there areas of tension, fatigue, or discomfort from standing, repetitive motions, or long hours? What does your body need right now?

Emotional Temperature

Rate your emotional state today (1-10). What emotions are you experiencing? Are you feeling energized, drained, inspired, overwhelmed, or something else entirely?

Boundaries and Balance

Reflect on your work-life boundaries this week. Are you overextending yourself? What boundaries do you need to set or reinforce to protect your time and energy?

Restorative Activities

List 3-5 activities that genuinely restore your energy and bring you joy. When was the last time you engaged in each? Schedule time for at least one this week.

Creative Burnout Assessment

Are you experiencing any signs of creative burnout (lack of inspiration, emotional exhaustion, cynicism, reduced productivity)? What might be contributing to these feelings?

Self-Care Action Plan

Based on your reflections above, identify 2-3 specific self-care actions you will take this week. Be realistic and specific about when and how you'll implement them.

Gratitude for Self

Acknowledge something you appreciate about yourself today—a quality, an achievement, or simply your resilience in showing up. You deserve recognition too.

Remember: Self-care is not selfish—it's essential. You cannot pour from an empty cup. By nurturing yourself, you ensure you have the energy, creativity, and compassion to serve your clients at the highest level.
