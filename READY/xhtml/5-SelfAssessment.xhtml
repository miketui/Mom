Self-Assessment

This self-assessment is designed to help you reflect on your current journey as a freelance hairstylist.

Take your time to answer each question thoughtfully.

1. What initially drew you to the art of hairstyling?

What sparked your passion for this creative calling?

2. What are your biggest hopes, dreams, and aspirations for your craft and career?

3. Reflect on your current techniques and practices. In what areas do you feel you have room to grow?

4. How would you describe your current approach to connecting with clients' needs?

5. What does being a "conscious hairstylist" mean to you at this stage?

6. In what ways do you hope to make a positive impact on your clients and community?

7. What opportunities do you see for greater inclusivity and sustainability in the beauty industry?

8. What self-care practices do you currently use to nurture your well-being?

9. What mentors or educational resources do you draw upon for growth?

10. What are you most excited to learn and create through hairstyling?

Honesty is the first step to transformation.
